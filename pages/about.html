Welcome to Roaming Shutter

Roaming Shutter is a travel photography blog created by me, Rakesh Jeyaraman.

I have always had a passion for photography and travel, and I started this blog as a way to share my experiences with others. Through my photos and stories, I hope to inspire others to explore the world and capture its beauty.

Whether you're a fellow traveler or just someone who loves photography, I hope you'll find something here that inspires you.

About Rakesh Jeyaraman

Hi there! My name is Rakesh Jeyaraman and I'm a travel photographer based in Atlanta. I started Roaming Shutter to share my love for travel and photography with the world.

I've always been passionate about exploring new places and capturing the beauty of the world through my lens. My travels have taken me to some amazing locations, from the beaches of Cancun to the mountains of Alps.

Through Roaming Shutter, I hope to inspire others to explore the world and appreciate its beauty. Whether you're an experienced photographer or just starting out, I believe that everyone has the ability to capture amazing images.

Thanks for visiting Roaming Shutter and I hope you enjoy my photos!
